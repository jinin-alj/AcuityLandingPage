The Problem

Students are burning out and they don't even notice.

76%

of college students report high or moderate stress levels during their academic journey.

77%

40%

of Gen Z feel stressed all or most of the time, not just during exam season.

The Solution

Adaptive guidance.
At the right moment.

Acuity reads your cognitive state using passive signals without any wearables or interruptions, so you always know when to push and when to rest.

01

Passive Signal Detection

Works quietly in the background, reading behavioral patterns to understand when your focus is genuinely fading, before you even notice it yourself.

02

Right-Timed Breaks

Breaks that come too late cost more than the time they take. Acuity replaces emotional guesswork with precise, data-driven cues delivered exactly when needed.

03

Personalized Rhythm

Adapts to exam seasons, your peak hours, and task type. It understands a deep coding session and flashcard review are not the same cognitive load.

04

Transparent by Design

Your data, your control. Full visibility into what signals Acuity reads, and full transparency into why it makes the recommendations it does.
